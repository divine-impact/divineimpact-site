About
Who we are

Divine Impact is a small, focused operation that builds governance infrastructure for autonomous AI agents.

We're not a hype shop. We're operators who run production AI systems every day and package what we learn into tools other people can use. Our governance framework wasn't designed in a brainstorm — it was built one hard lesson at a time, from credential exposure incidents to sub-agent deployment failures.

We believe the difference between an AI agent that works and one that breaks is governance — clear rules about what it can do, what it can't, how it escalates, and how you stay in control as it scales.
